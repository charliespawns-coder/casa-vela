Getting Here

How to Reach Casa Vela

Just a 2.5 to 3-hour drive from Manila via SLEX. The journey itself is a beautiful road trip away from the city.

Search "Casa Vela Cavinti" on Waze for directions. Here's the recommended route:

  1. 1 Take the South Luzon Expressway (SLEX) southbound.
  2. 2 Exit at Turbina-Real (not Calamba) to avoid heavy traffic in the Calamba Pansol area.
  3. 3 Look for the Pagsanjan Arc — you're less than 30 minutes away from Casa Vela.
  4. 4 Pass by Aling Taleng's famous Halo-Halo and take the road on the left.
  5. 5 Follow Waze via the Barangay Layasin route, or take the shortcut via Barangay Anglas Blue Chapel (turn right).
  6. 6 At the shortcut forks: turn LEFT at the first fork, then RIGHT at the last fork. Casa Vela will be less than 300m on your right.

Nearby Attractions

If you're looking for things to do in Cavinti, Laguna, Casa Vela is a solid base for waterfalls, lakes, cave tours, and countryside day trips around the area.

Pagsanjan Falls

Thrilling boat ride through Pagsanjan Gorge to one of the most famous waterfalls in the Philippines.

🌊

Hulugan Falls

A hidden gem — trek through lush forests to reach this majestic waterfall.

🏞️

Caliraya Lake

Scenic man-made reservoir with fishing, boating, kayaking, and lakeside picnics.

🦇

Cavinti Underground River

Spelunking adventure through fascinating cave formations and crystal-clear underground waters.

🌳

Lumot Lake

Also known as Sierra Lake — a quiet spot for fishing or boating.

🧗

Bumbungan Eco-Park

Ziplining, rappelling, trekking, and natural beauty — a full day of adventure awaits.

Cavinti Church

Visit the historic Saint Peter of Alcantara Parish Church — beautiful architecture and rich history.
